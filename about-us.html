Our History

Over the past 14years, ILEM has directly worked with 11 United Nations Agencies/Programmes; MRU-Secretariat; USAID West Africa Biodiversity and Climate Change Programme; IPRM; EAC-Secretariat, EU Delegation, Hivos, and ENERGIA.

Our Mission

To leverage technical assistance to governments and development partners through the formulation of responsive policies and implementation of tailor-designed programmes addressing environmental challenges, improving institutional delivery, and securing the wellbeing of people in Africa.

Company Overview - Vision

To be a premier action-driven entity leveraging specialized technical assistance addressing recurring environmental challenges, responding to human capital development needs, and strengthening institutional delivery for the wellbeing of the African society
